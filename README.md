## 📌 Overview
This Power BI project provides an interactive analysis of financial sales data, helping businesses monitor sales performance, profitability, product performance, and customer segments across different countries.

## 🎯 Key Metrics
- 💰 Total Sales: 119M
- 📈 Total Profit: 17M
- 📦 Total Units Sold: 1M
- 🏷️ Total Discounts: 9M

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
- Gross Sales vs Profit Comparison
- Sales by Country Analysis
- Profit by Country Analysis
- Sales & Profit by Product
- Profit by Discount Band
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Interactive Filters and Slicers

## 💡 Key Insights
- USA generated the highest sales among all countries.
- Government segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Paseo emerged as the top-performing product.
- Lower discount bands delivered higher profitability.
- Strong sales growth was observed during the last quarter of the year.
